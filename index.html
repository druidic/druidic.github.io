Technology that works for you

Druidic is a new kind of technology company. Actually, let's be honest, it's not really a company. It's just me. And I'm not interested in taking your money. I have a day job.

You know how apps and websites always seem to be trying to distract you, upsell you, or make you keep coming back just so they can show you more ads? Druidic doesn't do that. We have one goal, and one only: make beautiful, useful things.

The computer that runs in your web browser

The Grove is a virtual personal computer—complete with an operating system, persistent file storage, and apps—that runs in web browsers like Chrome and Firefox.

Say what? But it really works. You're using a web browser right now, so trying it out is as easy as clicking this link.

Did you try it? Cool. You now have a unified computing environment that fits on a thumb drive, that you can carry around between physical machines and run anywhere, that will outlive operating system upgrades and hardware failures and the caprices of app developers. You can use it without a mouse. You can use it on a tiny screen. You can customize it to your heart's content. No third parties will ever intrude to mess it up, or mine your data to show you ads. You'd have to do extra work to avoid making backup copies. This stupid little pretend machine will be with you till the day you die. Unless you delete it. Which is also easy.

Oh, do you know how to program in JavaScript?

Want to learn?

Programming on the Grove is one of the easiest ways to learn how to code. Unlike most tutorial environments, its capabilities keep pace with yours. It will take you from writing your first line of code to making full-fledged web applications.
