Enter the Grove.

The Grove is an uncomplicated computer, simulated in your browser. Optimized for writers, coders, and people who love tech from the ‘80s.

Try it out.

It does what your browser doesn't.

Whether it's Firefox, Chrome, or Safari, you do practically everything inside a web browser these days. But there are some things that web browsers aren't good at yet. Here's what the Grove can do that's missing from your current web experience:

  • Works without internet access
  • Saves files locally on your computer, where they're safe from data breaches
  • Only updates when you tell it to.
  • Provides a simple yet powerful suite of programming tools

You can take it with you.

Your entire Grove system is stored in a single file on your hard drive. And because it only needs a web browser to run, you can take it anywhere: Mac, Windows, Linux, or even Chromebook. And it works exactly the same on every single platform. We've even made an effort to avoid keyboard shortcuts that could differ across platforms, so the muscle memory you build on one system will transfer effortlessly to the next.

This means you can migrate between systems at will. Did one computer manufacturer or OS vendor do something you don't like? You can change platforms and never look back. They have no power over you. You're free.
